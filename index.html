About me

Chief Executive Officer of Alphabet & Google.

Sundar Pichai, in full Pichai Sundararajan, (born July 12, 1972, Madras [now Chennai], Tamil Nadu, India), Indian-born American computer scientist and executive who was CEO of both Google, Inc. (2015– ), and its holding company, Alphabet Inc. (2019– )..
